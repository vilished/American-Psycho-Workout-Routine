The American Psycho Workout

Strength Training – Monday and Friday

  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 x 4-6 reps (2 mins rest)
  • Pull ups (add weight if necessary): 3 x 4-6 reps (2 mins rest)
  • Lateral Raises: 3 x 8-12 reps (60 seconds rest)
  • Standing Dumbbell Curls: 2 x 6-8 reps (90 seconds rest)
  • Bar Dips (add weight if necessary): 2 x 6-8 reps (90 seconds rest)

*Use a high-quality weight belt to prevent discomfort as you add weight.

Strategic Cardio Training and Abs – Wednesday and Saturday

  • High Intensity Interval Training: Perform on Treadmill, Stationary Bike, Elliptical, or Sprinting outside
  • Option 1: 30 seconds hard / 30 seconds easy x 12
  • Option 2: 60 seconds hard / 60 seconds easy x 8
  • Low Intensity Cardio: 15-30 minutes at a walking pace
  • Core Training
    • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 x 8-12 reps (90 seconds rest)
    • Kneeling Abs Wheel Rollouts: 2 sets x 8-12 reps (90 seconds rest)

Rest Days – Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday

Rest days are for recovery. Skip the gym and let your body recuperate for optimal performance.

Remember: a great physique is built through intense workouts, ample recovery, and solid nutrition.
